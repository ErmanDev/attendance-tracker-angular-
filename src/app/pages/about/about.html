Attendance Tracker
Attendance Tracker is a web application that helps you track your attendance. Wether you're a teacher or a student, it provides a simple and efficient way to monitor attendance.
FAQ's
What does Attendance Tracker help me do?
Attendance Tracker is built to record who was present for a class or session, keep a clear history over time, and give both instructors and students a straightforward place to review attendance without juggling spreadsheets or paper rolls.
How do teachers mark attendance?
Instructors open the session for the class they are teaching, see the roster for that group, and mark each learner present, absent, or late. Changes save to that session so the record stays tied to the correct date and subject.
Can students see their own attendance?
Yes. After signing in, students can view their attendance history for the courses they are enrolled in, so they always know how their participation is recorded.
How are classes and rosters organized?
Each class has its own roster of students. When you take attendance, you are working against that roster for a specific meeting time, which keeps reports accurate and easy to filter by class or time period.
